How to Use the System

1. Start Recording. Use the Start Recording button to start a new recording. For quality assurance, the video should be at least 30secs and not more than 1min:30secs, to give a quality result. Also, the video should be taken at close proximity to where the sound is originating from or at a point of interest relative to the place the sound is originating from.

2. Stop Recording and Analyse. When done recording, Click the "Stop Recording and Analyse" button to save the recording to your local device and analyse the audio. After the analysis is complete, the system will take you to result page showing the result of the analysis.
